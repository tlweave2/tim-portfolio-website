About Timothy Weaver

Computer Science Student & Former Entrepreneur

Hey there! I'm Timothy Weaver, a passionate computer science student with a unique background in entrepreneurship. Currently pursuing my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at Arizona State University.

My journey is a bit unconventional - before diving into the world of code, I spent nearly two decades as the proud owner of Strokers, a successful motorcycle shop in Merced, CA. This experience wasn't just about bikes; it was a crash course in entrepreneurship, leadership, and problem-solving.

From Wrenches to Keyboards

The transition from motorcycles to computer science might seem unexpected, but for me, it was a natural progression. Both fields require analytical thinking, attention to detail, and a passion for building and fixing things. The thrill of solving complex problems and creating something from scratch – whether it's a custom bike or a new application – drives me every day.

My academic journey is equipping me with core programming principles, computer systems fundamentals, algorithms, data structures, and software development methodologies. But it's my real-world experience that sets me apart. I've honed my abilities in inventory management, customer service, financial planning, marketing, and team leadership - all skills that translate beautifully into the tech world.

Beyond the Screen

When I'm not immersed in code, you'll find me:

  • Hitting the trails
  • Planning my next travel adventure
  • Tinkering with personal coding projects
  • Enjoying a good cup of coffee while brainstorming new ideas

I'm a strong advocate for teamwork and collaboration. I believe effective communication and a collaborative spirit are key to achieving success in any project. As a lifelong learner, I'm constantly expanding my knowledge and skillset within the software engineering domain.

I'm excited to bring my unique blend of entrepreneurial spirit and technical skills to the world of computer science and software development. Let's connect and create something amazing together!
